My Work

Let's start with the site you're on right now! It's my current after-hours project to refresh my old website and show off some new tricks. I finally started using GitHub so you can actually go find this site there!

Currently I'm working at Horsepower Automotive Group as the sole web developer on our marketing team. Our sites I've worked on include Addictive Desert Designs, DV8 Offroad, Rago Fabrication, and Flatline Van Co, as well as B2B sites for our associated dealers and builders. Go check those sites out — I've done a lot of cool stuff on them!

My Responsibilities:

  • Developing custom themes and theme modifications with Shopify Liquid via Shopify CLI.
  • Creating custom meta fields and scripts for implementing new features and improving customer experience.
  • Developed AJAX functionality with Shopify's Cart API to make innovative new purchase flows.
  • Search Engine Optimization with Lighthouse, SEM Rush, Google Analytics, and Google Search Console.
  • Optimizing images, video, and code performance for enduser experience.
  • Experience in writing both product and article content.
  • Developing both media quality & data guidelines for uploading new products to each brand.
  • Maintaining brand data for SEMA Data Co-Op.

I've been with HPAG for nearly 2 years and have grown a tremendous amount here as a developer, marketer, and merchandiser. I'm an every day collaborator with our marketing/design team, new product team, and brand managment.

Still waiting for my FVC sticker, but I rep our brands on the Forester.

HPAG has also enabled me to use some of my other creative skills in photography, graphic design, videoography, 3D modeling, and more. These are all used extensively across the different brand sites to enhance the customer experience, customer understanding, and brand reputation.
